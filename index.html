Sergey Karev 

My basic medium is steel, I like it's hardness and plasticity.
I weave forms together with meanings and make them curl, and then they
crystalize into static objects. It may be seen as exploring the natural in its cultural reflections and the social in the shape of the sensual..  It’s a kind of sculptural dreaming: the thread can’t always be taken, but it is always something winding around.
